**ProductSense AI**
ProductSense AI is an AI-powered product management thinking coach that helps users structure product decisions using real PM frameworks.

**What it Does**
1.Guides product problem framing and user segmentation
2.Evaluates solution tradeoffs and prioritization
3.Helps define MVP scope and success metrics
4.Identifies risks and proposes experiments
5.Offers mock PM interview practice

**Tech Stack**
1.OpenAI GPT-4.1
2.Pinecone (RAG knowledge base)
3.Next.js frontend
4.Vercel hosting

**Live Demo**: https://my-ai-3-assistant-7fkc.vercel.app/

**Disclaimer**
ProductSense AI provides general product-thinking guidance only and may be inaccurate. It is not professional advice. Do not share sensitive information. Use at your own risk.
